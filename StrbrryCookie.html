Strawberry Cookies

if you are a strawberry lover, then these strawberry cookies are going to leave you stunned. Made from fresh chopped strawberries, these cookies are a delight to have. Ditch your chocolate cookies or vanilla cookies because strawberry cookies are here to stay and slay. You can have these with your morning tea or as evening snacks. Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 135 gm chopped strawberry
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 105 gm granulated sugar
  • 215 gm all purpose flour
