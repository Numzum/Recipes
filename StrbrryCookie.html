Strawberry Cookies

if you are a strawberry lover, then these strawberry cookies are going to leave you stunned. Made from fresh chopped strawberries, these cookies are a delight to have. Ditch your chocolate cookies or vanilla cookies because strawberry cookies are here to stay and slay. You can have these with your morning tea or as evening snacks. Your friends are surely going to ask you the recipe for this cookie as these fruity cookies will woo everyone with their lip-smacking f

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 135 gm chopped strawberry
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 105 gm granulated sugar
  • 215 gm all purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Whisk the ingredients

    2. First, preheat the oven to 180 degrees C. Now, in a bowl, add egg, oil, sugar and vanilla extract. Whisk to mix everything well.

  2. Prepare the dough

    3. Add baking powder and flour to the mixture and mix well. Once done, add chopped strawberries and mix again to prepare the cookie dough.

  3. Form round balls

    4. Pinch out small portions from the dough and start forming round balls. Now place them on the cookie sheets and sprinkle a bit of sugar on top.

  4. Bake the cookies

    5. Now, just bake the cookies for about 15 minutes.

  5. Your Strawberry Cookies are ready

    6. Your Strawberry Cookies are now ready to be served. Enjoy!
